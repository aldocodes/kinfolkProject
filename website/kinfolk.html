Background

From the Santa Ana riverbed, the red A-shaped structure of the Angel Stadium is visible, as is the night sky, filled with Disneyland fireworks. Cyclists are often rushing past. You wouldn't guess there are 440 homeless individuals living along the 30-mile trail stretching from Huntington Beach to the border of Orange and Riverside counties. An estimated 26% of homeless adults staying in shelters live with serious mental illness and an estimated 46% live with severe mental illness and/or substance use disorders.
